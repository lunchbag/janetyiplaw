MR. HUNGRY

- 9 -

"Over here!" said the voice.

Mr. Hungry looked to his side and saw a little worm sitting on his left ear. "Who are you?" asked Mr. Hungry.

 

- 10 -

"My name is Tony Tapeworm," said the little worm as he jumped off Mr. Hungry's ear and on to the ground. "I have been living in your belly for a long time now."
