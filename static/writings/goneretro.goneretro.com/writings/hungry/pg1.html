MR. HUNGRY

- 1 -

It was said that a long time ago, there was a very strange place in the world called Sweden.

In Sweden, the sun was very hot and it never rained. Every day was a beautiful day.

 

- 2 -

In Sweden lived a lot of strange animals. There were scaly lizards that could stand up and walk around, big elephants with curly curvy tusks, and bats with long sharp beaks.
