MR. HUNGRY

- 7 -

But after walking around for a long time, Mr. Hungry still could not find anything to eat.

He had eaten everything in Sweden!

Mr. Hungry started to become VERY hungry!

 

- 8 -

Soon, Mr. Hungry was so hungry, his belly began to hurt.

He started to cry, and suddenly heard a small voice.

"I'm hungry!" said the voice.

"Hello?" asked Mr. Hungry. He looked around but could not see anybody.
