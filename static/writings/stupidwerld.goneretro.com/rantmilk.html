Now Everyone Can Drink Milk

I used to hate milk. Especially warm milk. I'd want to vomit just thinking about the skin that forms on top.

I knew that milk was supposed to be healthy beyond belief though. So I tried. But I just hated it too much.

Then my school started this milk program. I had one chocolate milk and I was hooked. On to choc milk that is, I still hated regular milk.

But it was a start. I drank choc milk day and night. Soon, I was addicted. Couldn't have breakfast or sleep without it.

One day, the chocolate syrup ran out. There was nothing to go with my peanut butter sandwich! Oh, what to do...

So I tried regular milk. And it actually tasted normal. I didn't especially like it, but at least I could stand the taste.

With the knowledge that it was healthy urging me on, I started drinking white milk. The taste seemed to get better each passing day. Soon, I was in love with the gross flavour I once despised.

Now, I love milk. I drink it every day - without the chocolate. And looking thru old school pictures, I can see the difference. I'm still pretty weird looking right now, but I was definitely much worse before my milk addiction.

Now everyone can drink milk - and like it. Just do what I did.

I'm so proud of myself.
