Oranges

God, oranges are good. I'm eating one right now.

It tastes so good, a hint of sourness, a hint of sweetness. Fun to chew on.

There are two ways I like to eat them. First method is to just dump the whole thing into your mouth, and splash out the juice onto your tongue with your molars.

Or, second method is to use your front teeth to peel away the skin that covers an individual slice. Eat the skin. Then, carefully eat away the bottom of the slice so you don't let any juice leak out. You should be left with the stuff holding the orange juice (forgot its proper name.) Using little bites, eat each individual pulp thing.

I got orange juice on my space bar key while I was typing this out.

God, this tastes good. I think I'll go get another one.
