A Story

by Jennifer Y

This is a story my 8-year old sister wrote in about 1997.

Once a man was driving his car to the supermarket. When they arrived, the baby in his car was sleeping so the man didn't want to disturb him so he left the baby in the car and went to the supermarket.

When the man came back, he saw that all the window's glass had been broken and the baby had been kidnapped. So don't leave your baby or your kids in the car alone.

The End
