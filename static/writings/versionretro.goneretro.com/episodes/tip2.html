The Story of Janet's Second Tip
By Janet (08/13/01)
On Wednesday, I received my second tip from my non-tipping waitressing job. (My first tip was a few weeks earlier, it was $5 for a $35 bottle of wine; my third tip was yesterday, $3 for a $34 bottle of wine. But that's not the story here.)

We were closing, and I was aimlessly walking around, tired from the ten-hour work day I was subjected to.

The cleaning lady, (a very sweet woman with whom I had conversed with earlier that day,) was vacuuming. While she was doing so, she found a dime on the floor.

Well, now you can guess where this is heading.

She put it in my apron pocket and said "A tip for you, sweetie."

I didn't forget to say thanks.
