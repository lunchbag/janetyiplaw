MR. HUNGRY

- 5 -

Mr. Hungry loved to eat and he ate everything he saw. He ate apples, he ate grass, he ate fish, and he ate birds. He would even eat something that he picked up from off the ground!

 

- 6 -

One day, Mr. Hungry woke up feeling hungry as usual. He was very excited at the thought of going outside to eat.
