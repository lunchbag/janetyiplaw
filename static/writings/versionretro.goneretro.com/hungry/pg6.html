MR. HUNGRY

- 11 -

"So you have been eating all the food that I was eating?" asked Mr. Hungry.

"That's right!" said Tony Tapeworm. "That's why you were always so hungry no matter how much you ate!"

 

- 12 -

"But now, we have no more food left!" said Mr. Hungry. "We have eaten everything there is in Sweden!"

"I know," said Tony Tapeworm, "I am growing very hungry. In fact, I have even started to eat the inside of your belly!"
