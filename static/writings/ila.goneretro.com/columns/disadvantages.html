Disadvantages of an Imaginary Lover

Since the publishing of the Advantages piece, it's only fair.

  • They're not real.
  • The line "You better not touch me or my boyfriend will kick your ass!" isn't as effective.
  • Your mother probably wouldn't approve.
  • The Pope probably doesn't approve.
  • It's illegal to marry them in most places.
  • If you try to jump into their arms in a fit of romantic joy, most of the time, you'll fall on your face.
  • They'll never buy you dinner.
  • They're horribly un-photogenic.
  • Close-minded people think you're insane.
  • No matter what you tell yourself, they won't hold and kiss you... and they won't be there for you... and they don't really love you... and... ohmygodican'tcontinue
