Lover Archetypes

Here are some common patterns that most lovers, real or imaginary, will follow.

The Naive Schoolboy

Description:
Obsessively girl-crazy, he'll go after anything pretty, using cheap pickup lines he's heard on tv. When finally presented with a 'yes', he'll become so overjoyed and excited, that he'll clumsily walk into a pole, creating a humourous noise of some sort.

As a lover:
His adolescent hormones will make him eager to go far, but during the afterglow, he'll suddenly become frightened about the consequences he's not ready to handle. He'll make an excuse to go get something from the store but he won't come back. He'll avoid you the next day at school.


Example: Archie Andrews (Character in the Archie comic book series)

The Queer Hidden-Hero

 

Description:
He's the shy kid who everyone thought was gay until he rescued his entire class from the burning school. However, his fame is his demise, and his huge inflated ego has started to be increasingly annoying.

As a lover:
He needs to feel in control all the time, and will want to have everything his way. He's also got several weird fetishes, particularly the costume bit.


Example: Superman (Superhero)

The Intellectual Bore

 

Description:
He's a relentless perfectionist who wears the same colours every day. He holds an excellent level of hygiene.

As a lover:
He's actually alright in this category. However, after you're done, he'll criticize everything that went wrong and outline future plans to avoid such errors.


Example: Al Gore (Former American vice-president)

The Femme Fatale

 

Description:
She's mysteriously alluring, and even though you know you shouldn't, you can't help but follow her when she beckons.

As a lover:
You've fantasized about her for as long as you can imagine. But no one lives long enough to find out how she really is.


Example: The Blair Witch (Witch in Maryland, US )

The Fob Fatale

 

Description:
This handsome Chinese stranger looks alluringly mysterious, with his thick neon-blue rimmed glasses and coloured hair. His bright tight shirts and baggy flares do plenty to respectively compliment his lack of muscle and height.

As a lover:
He'll do anything you ask, because he's only sleeping with you for your passport. Even if he blatantly reveals this to you though, you won't be able to understand his English.


Example: Wang Li Ho (Ex-boyfriend of Sheetal)

The Eccentric Geek

 

Description:
He wears t-shirts with hardware manufacturer logos and possesses a brilliant wit that, unfortuantely, too few people understand.

As a lover:
Although he's done everything possible with his computer, because of his lack of experience with real people, he'll be clueless as to what to do. He'll search the internet for sex guides, but will not have the courage to go through with its bold suggestions. In the end, your baby will be conceived via in-vitro fertilization.


Example: Bill Gates (Founder of Microsoft)

The Beefy American Stud

 

Description:
He's big and strong, manly and beefy. You won't be able to take him in a single bite.

As a lover:
Being a football player, he'll see the bed as a field where he needs to be quick, rough and crowd-pleasing. However, outside the sporting area, he doesn't have the intellect to be good for anything else.


Example: Big Mac (McDonald's hamburger)

The Whiny Control Freak

 

Description:
He's the rich spoiled brat who's used to having his own way.

As a lover:
He's stubborn and outspoken about what he wants, and if you don't do what he says, he'll pay someone to set your house on fire. He's also very jealous and will go to great lengths to get you to stay home when he's away. (He may break your legs to confine you to a wheelchair or tell you lies that there are brain-eating zombies outside.)


Example: Adolf Hitler (German dictator)

The Casanova

 

Description:
Often with an accent but with sleazy undertones, this smooth-talker will start by taking you out for brunch, then charm you right into the sack.

As a lover:
He's very experienced and enjoys what he does. He may be forceful and stubborn about his priorities before yours. He's gone by 2am the latest. If you look for his name in the phone book the next day, you won't find it.


Example: Italy (European country)

The Sensitive Gentleman

 

Description:
You've never really paid much attention to him until your puppy dog got hit by a car and he took you to a hospital and comforted you. From then on, you start to notice the beautiful poems and stories he writes in class.

As a lover:
He gives excellent massages that will take away any backache. Never taking advantage of your insecurities, he won't do anything until you're absolutely sure it's what you want. Always considerate, he'll stop every few minutes to ask if you're still alright. Not only is he still there in the morning, but he'll have breakfast ready as well.


Example: Jesus Christ (Saviour)

The Wandering Husband

 

Description:
You are in a stable relationship with him, but you suspect that he may be getting bored. He visits other people's homes and often stays out the entire night. After spending all of his time and money on strangers, he has nothing left for you.

As a lover:
You vaguely remember happy times when you first met him, but too much time has passed since then.


Example: Santa Claus (Dead saint rumoured to be roaming)

The Whore

 

Description:
Being a large hoofed mammal, it has a short-haired coat, a long mane, and a long tail. The Whore (Equus caballus) has been domesticated since ancient times and is used for riding, and for drawing or carrying loads.

As a lover:
It's illegal to do it with a whore.


Example: Horse (Whore)
