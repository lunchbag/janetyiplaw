Advantages of an Imaginary Lover

Because of the huge amount of skepticism, this little list was needed. It'll constantly be added to as I think of more and more wonderful reasons for the ILA lifestyle.

  • They'll never leave you.
  • No risk of pregnancy or STD's.
  • He's sterile.
  • When you go out for dinner, the meal's always half-price.
  • They'll always do what you want them to do.
  • You have all the control of the relationship.
