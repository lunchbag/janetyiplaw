Stupid People 3:
Bottled Drinks
- - Janet [11/04/01]

Disclaimer: If you recognize anything you read here and you get offended, then I'm sincerely sorry, since Destiny's Child tells me that I shouldn't "diss you on the internet, cuz my mama told me better than that".

One time last year, Darren* and I were at Shoppers Drug Mart, and he wanted a drink. He decided to get one of those queer $3 drinks in colored themed bottles. He picked a Pina Colada drink.

Now, I don't like coconut, so I tried to discourage him with the very weak claim that it probably had alcohol in it. He said that he didn't think so.

We walked around for a bit, and when we were in the checkout line, I noticed that he had switched his drink to some weird fruit punch thing. I asked him his reason for the change.

"You were right, it DID have alcohol in it." he said.

"What?? How do you know?" I asked.

"It said on the bottle. 'Sand's Alcohol'."
