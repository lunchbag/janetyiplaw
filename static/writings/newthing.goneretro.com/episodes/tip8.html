Tip Stories 8:
Corruption
- - Janet [10/06/01]

On September 20th, I received my eighth tip.

It was an Italian function, and everyone wanted Espresso with sambuca. There was an Espresso machine at the front, and a table wanted me to bring them ten cups.

However, when I got to the front, I found that the machine took its time in spitting out the juice, and there was a crowd of people in line. "No problem," I thought, "I'm being paid to be here; I can afford to wait."

After a half hour though, I realized it was getting ridiculous. I went back to the table and explained to the ringleader that I couldn't bring them the coffee because it would take too long.

So he gets this knowing smile on his face, takes out his wallet, and hands me a $5. "Now, can we have some Espresso please?"

This other guy walked by and asked in a mock-shocked tone "Hey, what's going on here?"

He responded innocently "What? I'm giving her a tip."

So of course, I went back to the machine and waited. It was actually alright, because there was these two really nice gentlemen who kept complimenting me and helped me with getting the coffee.

And that's how I got my eighth tip, as corrupted as it was.
