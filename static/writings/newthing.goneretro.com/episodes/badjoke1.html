Janet's Bad Joke
- - Janet [08/13/01]

Yesterday, I told a really bad joke.

I was at work, scheduled to work from noon until 4pm. However, we were running late, so at 5pm, I was still there. I decided to call my mother to tell her I'd be late in coming home, since she was probably waiting for me to start dinner.

So I went into the kitchen and telephoned her. I told her I estimated that I'd be home around 5:30. One of the cooks happened to be standing beside me and overheard.

He said "5:30? Are you sure you'll be finished by then?"

I said "Sure, we don't have too much left to do."

Well, as it turned out, I wasn't done until 6:30. As I was leaving, the same cook asked me what happened to 5:30.

Now here comes the punchline...

"Yeah well, I meant Pacific time."

(Epilogue: The worst part was that I didn't even get the time zone right. I should've said Central time, which is one hour behind us. So not only was it a bad joke, it was inaccurate as well.)
