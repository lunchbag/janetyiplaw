The Birth of The Losers Culb
Feb 25, 2003
4:04 PM

From Tausif's suggestions, I'm gonna start documenting this whole process for the Losers Culb. Some of the stuff we talked about on icq is good, but I'm at work and using that portable icq program, and so is Tausif. That means even if Tausif has the history turned on, he can't copy-and-paste, or even select text. In the end, we decided that he was going to take screenshots of the history and email them to me, and I'll retype it. Yep.

But basically, it seems like Ian and Tausif are keen on the ideas of The Loser Culb and The Dirty Thief Club. The Loser Culb will be about being pathetic and spineless, and The Dirty Thief Club will be about being bastards and jerks. We are planning for them to be partners, and we imagine for them to have various territorial feuds in the future. The DTC will mercilessly taunt the LC, and the LC will have insults that vaguely make sense but don't.

Originally, I was just going to put together a "Loser Fish Club", because fish are funny. But then Tausif pointed out that the word 'fish' can have other connotations, which he didn't mind, but I did. So after going through a bunch of his suggestions, including "Black Rebel Losers Club", "Losers Fake Motorcycle Club", "Losers Run Slide Club, and "Uniciders Anonymous", we settled on a short-and-simple "The Club", or better yet, "The Culb".
